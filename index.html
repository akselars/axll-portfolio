Creating Digital Worlds

I'm AxLL and I specialize in detailed 3D environments, props, and objects. From cozy interiors to expansive landscapes, bringing virtual spaces to life with attention to every detail.

About Me

As a 3D artist with two years of experience, I've developed a passion for creating immersive environments and detailed objects. My journey in 3D modeling has been focused on crafting spaces that tell stories through their details and atmosphere.

Specialties

  • Environmental Design: Creating atmospheric scenes from cozy interiors to outdoor landscapes
  • Prop Creation: Crafting detailed objects and props that enhance scene authenticity
  • Scene Composition: Arranging elements to create visually compelling environments
  • Lighting & Mood: Setting the right atmosphere through careful lighting design

Tools I Use

  • Blender: Primary tool for 3D modeling, texturing, scene composition, and final renders
  • Unity: Basic knowledge for game development and interactive environments
